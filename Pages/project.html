A mobile app that tracks your personal spending with an AI right by your side! We targeted students and young adults to help them understand the importance of their finances and how to save better. WealthWise AI offers a wide range of topics to guide smart financial decisions and features an AI chatbot that gives personalized advice based on your spending habits. It can even answer questions like “What was my biggest expense this month?”

Out of over 250 projects, this one placed in the Top 20 at Florida's largest Hackathon, ShellHacks. This project was built with only 36-hours. I worked on the front-end using React Native and Expo, helping design a clean, simple, and user-friendly UI with vibrant orange buttons placed within thumbs' reach. I also contributed to the backend by testing API endpoints using Postman and helping build core functionalities.

Technologies used: React Native, Expo, JavaScript, Node.js, Postman, and Figma.
